HIGHLINE:

Highline is a truly unique concept bringing special attention to the importance of quality and freedom of design. The concept consists of two different types of textile flooring solutions: broadloom and tiles.Every Highline solution can be created in different qualities to match your budget and demands for wear-resistance, acoustics, comfort, insulation, fire resistance, etc.Highline can be further classified under different categories for your assistance.

  • Cityscapes
  • Modular Express

Canvas Collage is designed by Dutch designer Nicolette Brunklaus. Her love of rich textile structures is obvious and the woven structure is applied to all variations of the collection, layered over dried dahlia flowers, in a patchwork of colours and combined with new forms.
Canvas Collage represents a collection of 5 designs that can be integrated. Each of the designs stands alone beautifully and when combined they seamlessly interlace with each other to create an atmosphere of effortless comfort in a hotel, office or home.

In Almanac Benjamin Webb and ege focus on the duality between nature and civilization. What connects the two things, and how modern man struggles to understand Mother Nature and the modern world. With two unique designs, the collection sets out to express, inspire and push the boundaries between on-grid and off-grid through a range of multilayered designs and colourways. It is about a duality of modern life and finding the balance. Almanac celebrates the relationship between the two realities of the pulsating capital city and the tranquility of the deep forests.
