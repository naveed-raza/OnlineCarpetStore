Our Products:

The Persian Inn Produce the Best Quality hand made Carpets from Pakistan at the most Competitive Prices. One of the largest Companies of Pakistan dealing in Hand Made Carpet was established 35 years ago. The company is well known all over the world for making of hand-knotted carpets in wide variety of designs and colors. With its vertical and horizontal integration the company has all facilities available for making of best quality hand made carpets. Given the large manufacturing capacity, The Persian Inn are able to produce an infinite variety of designs in all sizes and color combinations, in many types of qualities. We have wide variety of goods in our warehouse to meet the bulk orders. We also produce programmed goods at most competitive rates on the requirement of our customers.

  • Avia
